Food

The Burg

110 W 4th S, Rexburg, ID 83440

The Burg is a great place to get a yummy classic cheeseburger and delicious thick milkshakes. It was made by students for students, but everyone can enjoy a burger from The Burg.

Gator Jacks

160 W 2nd S, Rexburg, ID 83440

Take a trip to the southern swamp and enjoy a fresh sandwich from Gator Jacks. It a make your own sandwich place and you can get a chance to try some alligator bites. This is the place for unique yummy sandwiches.

Taqueria El Rancho

312 W 4th S, Rexburg, ID 83440

Get a taste of authentic mexican food. Experience eating it in a bus or a sit down restaurant. Try their horchata its the best.
