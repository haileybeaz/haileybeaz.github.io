Project Purpose

To give information what is available to do in or near Rexburg, Idaho area.

The Audience

My main audience are student. Both male and female from the ages 18-27. It can also range to anyone that is unfamiliar to the area of Rexburg. Since students are my audience it will influence what kind of info on my websites. Usually students are low on money so they want things to do that doesn’t cost too much money. They also want things that they can do on a date or in a group of friends. The activities need to be close to the area because most students don’t have great modes of transportation.

Persona

This is a group of students at BYU-Idaho. They wanted to come to rexburg because of the price of tuition and the safe environment. They are excited for college life away from home. It's college so they want to have a fun time and not just hit the books. Their students and funding is low, so they want things that are close, but fun to do with friends.
