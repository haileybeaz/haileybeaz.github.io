Visitor Analysis

Target Audience

What are the Demographics?

  • Age: 25 and up
  • Income: High
  • Education: High
  • Geographic Location: Foreign

How did the site meet the needs of the visitor?

It gives information on travel sites in the United States. It gives a link to a free travel guide. Any person that wishes to visit the United States will want to know the best places to go.

Describe how the content was organized to facilitate it being found.

It has a search bar at th top if a person visiting the site wants to learn more about a specific spot in the united states. Then it is organized by destinations, trips, and experiences. This shows the prioties of the purpose of the site. They want the viewer to learn about the places they can go and then they can plan their trip to those places on the same site.

What did the site to make the mobile experience enjoyable?

I loved the simplicity and how it directed me to exactly what the viewer would want. The navigation wasn't too difficult and I could move myself around the site pretty well.

Did the site seem to facilitate people in a mobile environment?

Yes, I didn't have any issues with getting lost in th site. It minimized its information to what was important for the viewer.
