Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to provide weather information to the user. Specifically, weather information of their current location.

Audience Description

My Audience are people from the ages 20 to 50. Both male and female that are familiar with basic techonology. They love simplicity and easy mobility. They want a weather website to be easy to read and get their basic weather needs fast and quick. They would want to use a weather website to know what kind of day they will have and what they will need to prepare. For example, what clothes they will wear and how long will it take for them to get to work or class.

Persona

My persona is a 25 year old caucasian woman, Jane, from Toronto, Canada. She is in a relationship, but not married. She works for a major business company as foreign management. She works with other branches of their company located in several places around the world. She makes presentations to represent the company to make sure everyone is on the same page for goals. For her work she is tech savy. She is busy and always on a time crunch so she needs thing to be clear and fast. She believes in getting the job done well and quick. She is always traveling and needs to know the conditions outside. She has a laptop, but it is her mobile device that she has on her at all times. She usually does not spend much time broswing for long her phone. She likes to do things quick.
